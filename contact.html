Why a Franchise?

Every Franchisee need a successful franchisor and as one of the fastest growing tea demand in globally “Chai Chulha” could be what you are looking for.

Chai Chulha is a safe business, because no one knows the secret ingredients of our famous recipies. All the premixes are coming from Rajasthan and the staff is only taught to use the Premix packets, thus the business is always secure for the franchisee and he never has to worry about the competition.

#1. Franchise Requirements

Space requirement 300 Sq Ft- 500 Sq Ft Best Suggested Location Choice :- Shopping Ares / Mall food court / Airport / Near Crowded railway station area / Near Call center or IT park or College or park where footfall of general public is much.
Fund Requirement – Rs 10-15 Lac

#2. Benefit of Franchise

High Demand and High quality controlled product – Tea is available or can be made by even any one on footpaths stall also, but the taste and hygienic product with high quality control by highly qualified professionals bring the customer back to the shop of "Chai Chulha" which make us different from other and there is total secret of masala ingredients as they are in house manufactured at the centralized unit in Rajasthan, hence copy of our taste is just impossible.

#2. Advertisement and Marketing of Products

As a "Chai Chulha" franchisee, you will benefit from our strong franchise brand positioning and marketing expertise. Ongoing extensive advertising campaigns ensure that the Chai Chulha franchise brand achieves maximum awareness and drives maximum footfall.
